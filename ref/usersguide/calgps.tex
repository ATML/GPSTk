\section{\emph{calgps}}
\subsection{Usage}
\getuse{calgps --help}
\subsection{Examples}
\begin{Verbatim}
> calgps -3
\end{Verbatim}
\getuse{calgps -3}
>\begin{Verbatim}
> calgps -Y 1998
\end{Verbatim}
\getuse{calgps -Y 1998}
\subsection{Notes}
The -Y option will print the calendar for the entire year specified, you cannot specify a month.
